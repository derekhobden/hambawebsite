How It Works

Our simple three-step process to finding the right insurance

The Hambacompare Process

We've simplified the insurance comparison process to make it quick, easy, and respectful of your privacy.

1

Submit Application

Fill out a single application with your insurance requirements.

2

Get Quotes

Receive multiple quotes from South Africa's leading insurers.

3

Connect

Choose the quotes you like and connect directly with those insurers.

Step 1: Submit Your Application

The first step is to tell us what you're looking to insure. Our application process is designed to be straightforward and user-friendly.

Simple Form

Our application form is clear and easy to complete, asking only for the information that's necessary.

Multiple Insurance Types

Select from building, home contents, personal items, vehicle, and personal liability insurance.

Secure Process

Your information is encrypted and securely stored in compliance with the POPI Act.

Step 2: Get Your Quotes

Once you've submitted your application, our system works to gather quotes from multiple insurance providers.

Fast Results

Receive quotes quickly without having to visit multiple websites or make multiple phone calls.

Compare Side-by-Side

Easily compare coverage options, premiums, and benefits from different providers.

Transparent Information

See clear details about each quote, including what's covered and what's not.

Step 3: Connect With Insurers

The final step puts you in control of who contacts you. Only the insurers you select will receive your contact information.

Privacy Protected

Your contact details are only shared with the insurers you choose to connect with.

Direct Communication

Connect directly with the insurer to finalize your policy and ask specific questions.

No Obligation

You're under no obligation to purchase. Take your time to make the right decision.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hambacompare free to use?

Yes, Hambacompare is completely free for consumers. We receive a commission from insurance providers when you purchase a policy through our platform.

How many insurance providers do you compare?

We work with a wide range of South Africa's leading insurance providers to ensure you get comprehensive coverage options.

Will I be bombarded with calls from insurance companies?

No. Only the insurance providers you select will receive your contact information and be able to contact you.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All information is encrypted and stored securely in compliance with the POPI Act. Learn more on our POPI Act Compliance page.
