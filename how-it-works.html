Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hambacompare free to use?

Yes, Hambacompare is completely free for consumers. We receive fees from insurance companies for successful referrals. We never collect or handle any money from customers at any time.

Is Hambacompare an insurance company?

No, Hambacompare is not an insurance company. We are a comparison platform that helps you find and compare insurance quotes from various providers. The actual insurance policies are provided by the insurance companies themselves.

Is Hambacompare a broker?

No, Hambacompare is not a broker. We do not provide financial advice or sell insurance products. We simply collect your information and pass it on to the insurance providers that you specifically select to proceed with.

Who provides the insurance advice?

All financial and insurance advice is provided directly by the insurance companies. Hambacompare is not authorized to provide financial advice and does not do so. We recommend consulting with the insurance providers or a licensed financial advisor for specific advice.

How many insurance providers do you compare?

We work with a wide range of South Africa's leading insurance providers to ensure you get comprehensive coverage options.

Will I be bombarded with calls from insurance companies?

No. Only the insurance providers you select will receive your contact information and be able to contact you.

Is my contact information secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All information is encrypted and stored securely in compliance with the POPI Act. Learn more on our POPI Act Compliance page.
