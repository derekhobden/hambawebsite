Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours.

I'm having technical issues with the website. What should I do?

Please email us at info@hambacompare.com with details about the issue you're experiencing. Screenshots are always helpful.

Can I call Hambacompare directly?

We currently handle all customer inquiries through email. This allows us to efficiently route your query to the right team member.

I'm an insurance provider interested in partnering with Hambacompare. Who should I contact?

Please email partners@hambacompare.com. Our partnerships team will get back to you promptly.

I have questions about how my data is handled. Who should I contact?

For any privacy or data protection concerns, please email privacy@hambacompare.com. Our Information Officer will address your questions.
